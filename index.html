I2DS Tools for Data Science Workshop 21

About the workshop

This an online workshop on various tools and techniques for Data Science with R. All content was produced and prepared by students of the Master of Data Science for Public Policy, the Master of Public Policy, and the Master of International Affairs of the Hertie School, Germany. It is part of the course Introduction to Data Science.
