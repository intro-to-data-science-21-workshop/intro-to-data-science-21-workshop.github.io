I2DS Tools for Data Science Workshop 21

About

This an online workshop on various tools and techniques for Data Science with R. It was part of the course Introduction to Data Science (I2DS), run at the Hertie School, Germany. All content was produced and prepared by students of the Master of Data Science for Public Policy, the Master of Public Policy, and the Master of International Affairs. The live sessions of the workshop were held on November 4, 2021 (see here for the original schedule).
